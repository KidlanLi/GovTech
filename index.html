Climate Conflict: The Game

Story Background

Climate change is no longer a distant threat; it’s a reality that is reshaping societies worldwide. As resources dwindle and extreme weather events become frequent, communities face challenges in survival and cooperation.

Storytelling

Players will explore the impacts of climate change through a geospatial perspective, encountering real-world examples of conflicts exacerbated by environmental stressors.

Game Introduction

Climate Conflict is a strategy game where you manage limited resources to tackle climate disasters. Work as a team or compete to achieve your goals, while ensuring the global climate tracker doesn’t reach zero.
