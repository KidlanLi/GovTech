Climate Vulnerability Index (CVI)

The Climate Vulnerability Index measures a region's ability to adapt to the effects of climate change. It considers factors such as socioeconomic status, infrastructure, and environmental conditions to assess risk levels.

Conflict data for the year 2021

ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data) is a conflict data collection initiative. For the purpose of our project, we understand conflict as the combination of events (such as protests, attacks, bombings) and fatalities.
