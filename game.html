Game Rules

Objective: Achieve personal victory by fulfilling your role’s specific requirements or ensure the global climate tracker does not drop to zero, which would result in a shared loss.

Game Phases:

  1. Event Phase: Draw a random event card, which affects the climate tracker and players' resources.
    • Drought: Reduces water resources and decreases the climate tracker.
    • Flood: Destroys infrastructure and reduces water and food resources.
    • Heatwave: Increases food consumption for all players.
  2. Action Phase: Players act based on their roles:
    • Politician: Redistribute resources and mediate disputes.
    • Scientist: Predict the next event and propose solutions.
    • Farmer: Convert water into food resources.
    • Industrialist: Build infrastructure to mitigate disasters.
  3. Resolution Phase: The outcome of actions is calculated. Successful actions stabilize resources and climate; failures result in penalties.

Winning Conditions:

  • Individual Victory: Achieve your specific role objectives.
  • Collective Victory: Prevent the global climate tracker from reaching zero.
  • Shared Loss: If the tracker drops to zero, all players lose.
